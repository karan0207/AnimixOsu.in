Hunter X Hunter

Animation Action Adventure

Gon Freecss aspires to become a Hunter, an exceptional being capable of greatness. With his friends and his potential, he seeks out his father, who left him when he was younger.

Creator Yoshihiro Togashi
Stars
Issei Futamata Megumi Han Cristina Valenzuela
Hunter X Hunter - 148 Episode

Storyline

Gon Freecss is a young boy living on Whale Island. He learns from "Hunter" Kite, that his father, who he was told was dead, is still alive somewhere as a top "Hunter," risking his life to seek unknown items, such as hidden treasures, curiosa, exotic living creatures, etc. Gon decides to become a professional Hunter and leaves the island. To become a Hunter, he must pass the Hunter Examination, where he meets and befriends three other applicants: Kurapika, Leorio and Killua. Can Gon pass this formidable hurdle, the Hunter Examination, to become "the Best Hunter in the World" and eventually meet his father?

—Nippon Television Network

Best Arc's

1. The Zodyck Family Arc

The Zoldyck Family arc is exclusive to the anime. It kicks off with Gon, Leorio, and Kurapika traveling to Killua's family home deep within the Kukuroo mountain. This arc reveals the inner details of the Zoldyck household, especially the borderline-abusive manner in which they treat any child who steps out of line. Thankfully for Killua, he has a warm relationship with his father, Silva, although his interactions with his mother leave a lot to be desired.

2. The Heaven's Arena Arc

This is the first Hunter x Hunter arc to be set entirely inside an urban sprawl — the titular arena is a theoretically unfeasible building consisting of 251 floors. While Gon and Killua have a fun time taking down the weaker opponents in Heaven's Arena, they are struck speechless (and motionless) when they come face to face with Hisoka's extremely threatening Nen.

3. The Hunter Exam Arc

The first arc of Hunter x Hunter introduces all the main characters in a rather efficient manner. Gon meets Leorio and Kurapika on the ship taking them to the exam, which encounters a massive storm in the middle of the journey. The real exam is a lot more difficult, largely due to the sheer number of seemingly unrelated problems given to the aspiring Hunters.

4. The Yorknew City Arc

The Yorknew City arc is Kurapika's time to shine. It describes his suffering after the Phantom Troupe coldly murdered the Kurta clan in order to steal and sell their precious Scarlet Eyes on the black market. Kurapika captures one of the Troupe's members, Uvogin, after a long and rather perilous fight. At the same time, the Ten Dons hire Zeno and Silva Zoldyck, two of the world's best assassins, to kill Chrollo Lucilfer, the so-called head of the Spider.

5. The Greed Island Arc

The Greed Island arc is one of the most exciting ones in the anime. It involves Gon and Killua's adventures on Greed Island, a game invented by Ging and his friends. The intrepid pair experiences some difficulty at the beginning, as they have to fight advanced combatants, but the introduction of the fabulous Biscuit Krueger pretty much flips the game on its head.
